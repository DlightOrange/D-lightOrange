About Us

At D'light Orange, we are passionate about bringing you the freshest and most refreshing orange ice drinks. Our journey began with a simple idea among four friends: to create a delicious and natural beverage that everyone could enjoy. Together, Aditya, Andi Hanif, Siegfried, and Qolbiyah Alif combined their love for fresh flavors and dedication to quality to make this vision a reality. We believe in the power of simplicity, using only the finest, sun-ripened oranges to craft our signature drink. Every sip of D'light Orange is a celebration of flavor, freshness, and the joy of sharing moments with loved ones. Whether you're relaxing at home, on the go, or catching up with friends, our orange ice drinks are here to brighten your day.
Join us in our mission to spread happiness, one glass of D'light Orange at a time!

Our Product Excellence

At D'light Orange, our commitment to excellence starts with the product itself. We go above and beyond to ensure that each drink we serve meets the highest standards:

Quality

We source the finest oranges, ensuring every batch is fresh and bursting with natural flavor. Our commitment to quality is unmatched, and it shows in every sip.

Taste

Our signature recipe balances sweetness with the natural tang of oranges, creating a drink that is both refreshing and satisfying. It's the perfect way to beat the heat and enjoy a flavorful treat.

Freshness

We believe in the power of fresh ingredients. That's why our drinks are made with real oranges and contain no artificial flavors or preservatives, delivering a truly natural taste experience.

Customer Experience

Beyond just the product, D'light Orange is dedicated to providing a superior customer experience. We focus on the details that matter to our customers:

Price

We believe that great taste should be accessible to everyone. Our pricing strategy is designed to provide high-quality drinks at a price that everyone can enjoy, making D'light Orange a perfect choice for any budget.

Satisfaction

Our goal is to make every customer feel valued. From our friendly service to the consistent quality of our drinks, we strive to exceed your expectations. The positive feedback from our loyal customers is a testament to our dedication.
